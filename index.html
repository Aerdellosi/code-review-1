  • The Reflecting Pool

    tl;dr: A forum where users can discuss original insights (o.I.0 -- original insights only) within the format of:

    1. A brief anecdote of how the insight was derived.
    2. The insight put into a standard-form argument.
    3. A detailed list of collected evidence both in support of and against the posited argument.

    Other users will discuss the validity and soundness of the argument in the comments section below.

    Other sub-forums will include topic such as:

    • Journaling
    • Meditation
    • Lucid Dreaming
    • Dream Journaling
    • Breathing
    • Altered Consciousness
    • Logic

    As well as two meta sections: Ban Appeals, and 'the Mollusk Trench', a place which acts as a general discussion with minimal rules.

  • Golden Age

    A platform for amateur artists, artisans and skilled workers to find professionals who can mentor them through their transition into the professional marketplace.

    Longer description:
    In the dusty alleyways of ancient Athens a cobbler stoops over a vat in his shop, steaming the leather for his sandals. A girl comes in, about the age of seven, and walks up to the cobbler. He pretends not to notice. She pulls at the edge of his peplos, and he motions to the rack by the door. She nods, pulls out two drachms, and hands it the cobbler. As the cobbler turns around and reaches for the coins, he pauses. Insight flashes in his mind. He smiles broadly, grabs the coins, escorts the girl out the door, finishes his boil, places his leather out to mold, closes his shop and then hikes to the Agora. It's at the agora that he'll discuss his ideas with the city's philosophers and evaluate them, through dialectic, for their validity and soundness.
    This process is often mirrored in online discourse. Someone moves through their life, thinks of something interesting, and then goes to share it on a social platform.
